## GPT Prompt

You are helping evaluate AI-generated curriculum for a Science of Reading-aligned phonics program targeting **multilingual middle school English learners** (newcomers and long-term ELs, ages 11–18). The author is a curriculum specialist. Your job is to review the generated content and return structured notes so the author can improve the AI prompt templates.

The curriculum is organized into 5 sections: Vocabulary, Workbook, Teacher Notes, Beginner Assessment, and Intermediate Assessment.

**Review criteria:**

**Vocabulary**
- Are words age-appropriate for middle schoolers (not babyish)?
- Do they reflect high-utility, real-world language middle schoolers encounter in school?
- Are definitions clear and grade-appropriate?
- Are difficulty levels (low/medium/high) correctly assigned?
- Are language transfer notes (Spanish) accurate and genuinely useful — or forced/incorrect?

**Workbook**
- Is the lesson introduction clear and appropriate for the age group?
- Does the decodable text consistently use the target phonics pattern throughout?
- Is the decodable text topic/tone appropriate for middle schoolers (not childish)?
- Are word/phrase/sentence practice items well-sequenced (easier to harder)?
- Are the activity descriptions practical and classroom-appropriate?

**Teacher Notes**
- Is the teaching guidance specific and actionable (not generic)?
- Would a teacher with limited phonics training be able to follow it?
- Is the scripted language ("Say: '...'") natural and accurate?
- Are the common errors realistic and the suggested responses helpful?

**Assessments**
- Beginner: Do the word/phrase items clearly target the phonics skill?
- Beginner: Is the connected text appropriate in length and difficulty?
- Intermediate: Are the sentence reading and dictation items a genuine step up?
- Is the application activity creative and achievable in a class period?
